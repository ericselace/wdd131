Helping Hands Mission: we share love

1. Site Name

Helping Hands Mission

  • This name represents a charitable organization that brings together volunteers to serve in remote areas with needy people.
  • helpinghandsmission.org

2. Site Purpose

pour Helping Hands Mission

  • The site allows you to recruit volunteers, explain the proposed missions, share the needs of local communities, and provide information on the steps to take to participate in a mission.
  • It also includes a registration form for volunteers.

3. Scenarios

  • How can I volunteer and participate in a mission?
  • What are the next destinations and types of services requested?
  • How can I donate or financially support a mission?

4. Color Schema

  • Couleur principale : Vert foncé (#2E7D32) — utilisé pour les en-têtes, boutons, et éléments importants.
  • Couleur secondaire : Beige clair (#F1F8E9) — utilisée pour les arrière-plans et sections de contenu.
  • Accent : Orange doux (#FFA726) — utilisé pour attirer l’attention sur les appels à l’action.

5. Typography

  • Main title (h1, h2): 'Merriweather', serif — for a serious and professional style.
  • Body text : 'Open Sans', sans-serif — for smooth playback on all devices.
  • Special elements : 'Roboto Mono', monospace — used for block quotes or technical information.
